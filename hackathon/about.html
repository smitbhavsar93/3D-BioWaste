A prosthetist can make a simple prosthetic for any person. But with today's fashion trends and advancement in technology, prosthetics are evolving and getting more creative. We offer a wide range of blueprints designed according to today’s trends and fashion all in our cloud API, which can be accessible anywhere and anytime. It's just a one click to send it to a 3d printing company. There are more than 1 million annual limb amputations globally.
