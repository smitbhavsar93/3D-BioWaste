Our Sponsers

Meet The Companies That Have Partnered with 3D BioWaste



Coca-Cola

Coca-Cola, nicknamed the most polluted brand in the world in 2019, saw staggering numbers when it came to waste managing their plastic bottles. A staggering 91% of all Coca-Cola bottles were found to go to waste. According to, CBC News, nearly 3 million Coca-Cola bottles are being wasted yearly after a study was done in 2020, which amounts to nearly 200,000 bottles being thrown to waste every single minute. This is ridiculous and Coca-Cola are finally putting an end to this, so if you want to be as eco-friendly as Coca-Cola partner with us today!




Pepsi

When there's Coca-Cola in the conversation, Pepsico will always find its way in as well. Pepsi, along with Coca-Cola, also was one of the biggest waste manufacturers during the 2010s, reaching numbers as high as Coca-Cola. However, unlike Coca-Cola, Pepsico has openly acknowledged this and is now promising on cutting these numbers by nearly 50% by the year 2030.




Nestle

The final company that has decided to partner with us is Nestle. The situation with Nestle is slightly different from Coca-Cola and Pepsico. Unlike the other two companies, Nestle doesn't only serve bottles, they also come in the form of candy wrappers, cardboard ice cream boxes and many more. They produce as much waste as the other two and this is why they have partnered with us to try and reduce their footprint on this planet and help fight for a greater cause.
