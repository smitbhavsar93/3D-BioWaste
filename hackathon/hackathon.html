Waste Management for
Sustainable Environment

Here at 3D BioWaste, our goal is to reduce our planets plastic waste drastically and start using it towards our healthcare, more pecifically, towards prosthetics. According to (ADD SOURCE), nearly 3 million Coca-Cola bottles are being wasted yearly after a study was done in 2020, which amounts to nearly 200,000 bottles being thrown to waste every single minute. Coca-Cola isn't just the only criminal when it comes to producing plastic waste, but they are only 1 of thousands of businesses who do this regularly. This can't go on forever, as Earth only has a finite amount of resources available, change needs to happen soon, and where better to start than here, at 3D BioWaste. We will collect your waste than your company was gonna throw out anyway, and use it for the betterment of Earth's society.

3D Printing in the Medical Field

Day by day, as technology advances, so does medicine. Our goal at 3D BioWaste is to look into the future of prosthetics, which we believe will advance the human race. By taking wasted plastic and melting it down to its natural state, we can use this as filament for future 3D printing needs. Being more specific, for 3D printing prosthetics. In the future, we at 3D BioWaste believe that prosthetics could be vital for increasing human lifespan.
